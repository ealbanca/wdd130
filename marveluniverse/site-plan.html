Content

Home page

Welcome to MARVEL UNIVERSE. The perfect place to learn more about its characters, powers, and roles in this exiting universe. Martin Goodman, a publisher of comics was the one that got the idea to create a superhero world and bring excitement to fiction and action comic’s readers. Thanks to a very talented person called Stanley Lieber (known as Stan Lee) this idea became a reality back in the 1960s. The Fantastic Four was the first comic Stan Lee and his team created. He mentioned multiple times how he wanted to create normal people with unique powers. He also focused at all on day-to-day stories where the characters interacted in very common situations. It made teenagers and the public in general identify themselves with the comics. It became so popular that literally a Universe came to life. Nowadays, different publishers and creators have used the same characters to develop new stories, products, films, series and even comics that keep catching the attention of the audience. If You have read up to here, it’s because you’re a real MARVEL UNIVERSE fan. Take time to check our website to learn and love more this amazing UNIVERSE.

Heroes

Captain America, Hulk, Thor, and Iron Man are some of the most known Marvel Universe Heroes. Each of them has his own background, skills, and enemy. Here you will be able to learn more about some of the Heroes. CAPTAIN AMERICA: He is the Iconic America army hero. Identified as a fragile young man that decided to enlist in the army. He took part in a superhero experiment which gave him strength and resilience power. He became popular during the Second World War. The only one that can lift Thor’s hammer. IRON MAN: A rich man that invested his fortune on developing technology to protect humanity. Based on his knowledge and money, Tony Stark developed weapons, shields, shelters and even an army of robots. His exoskeleton made of iron and his heart ignited by energy, make him very special in the Universe Marvel. HULK: A big green and strong monster stronger than many of the marvel universe. Incredibly, he was a scientist that developed a formula guilty of his physical change. When Bruno gets mad, he quickly turns into the big green guy and smashes everything around him. There is only one person capable to get him under control.

Villains

Villains in the Marvel Universe are generally considered heroes by some population. It’s amazing to see how Stan Lee and all the contributors worked to make them as real as possible. Thanos and Loki are perfect examples of characters part of a world with clear desires, positions and purposes. We learn from the Marvel Universe that sometimes Villains are stronger than Superheros, and that makes the story more entertaining. Thanos: He is an Eternal Deviant from the moon Titan. A purple strong character that believes in the balance of the universe. He wants to save the worlds by eliminating half of their population. According to him, it will give those worlds a second chance to become better. During all his live, he works on compiling the Infinity stones to get his objective completed. Loki: Brother of Thor and Odin’s son (Which make sim a God) Loki is well known as the God of the lies. He has the power to disappear and impersonate others. He is very smart and is always looking for ways to be admired.
