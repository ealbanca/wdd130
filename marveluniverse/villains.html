Villains in the Marvel Universe are generally considered heroes by some population. It’s amazing to see how Stan Lee and all the contributors worked to make them as real as possible. Thanos and Loki are perfect examples of characters part of a world with clear desires, positions and purposes. Loki: Brother of Thor and Odin’s son (Which make sim a God) Loki is well known as the God of the lies. He has the power to disappear and impersonate others. He is very smart and is always looking for ways to be admired.

We learn from the Marvel Universe that sometimes Villains are stronger than Superheros, and that makes the story more entertaining. Thanos: He is an Eternal Deviant from the moon Titan. A purple strong character that believes in the balance of the universe. He wants to save the worlds by eliminating half of their population. According to him, it will give those worlds a second chance to become better. During all his live, he works on compiling the Infinity stones to get his objective completed.
