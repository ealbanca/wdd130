Marvel's Universe

Welcome to MARVEL UNIVERSE. The perfect place to learn more about its characters, powers, and roles in this exiting universe. Martin Goodman, a publisher of comics was the one that got the idea to create a superhero world and bring excitement to fiction and action comic’s readers. Thanks to a very talented person called Stanley Lieber (known as Stan Lee) this idea became a reality back in the 1960s. The Fantastic Four was the first comic Stan Lee and his team created. He mentioned multiple times how he wanted to create normal people with unique powers. He also focused at all on day-to-day stories where the characters interacted in very common situations.

It made teenagers and the public in general identify themselves with the comics. It became so popular that literally a Universe came to life. Nowadays, different publishers and creators have used the same characters to develop new stories, products, films, series and even comics that keep catching the attention of the audience. If You have read up to here, it’s because you’re a real MARVEL UNIVERSE fan. Take time to check our website to learn and love more this amazing UNIVERSE.
